About NeuroTech Robotics

Club Introduction

NeuroTech Robotics is a student‑led club dedicated to bridging the gap between Botswana and emerging technologies through hands‑on exploration of robotics.

Our Core Values

Impact, Teamwork, Fun, Innovation, and Discovery. Guided by these values and our slogan “Wired by Innovation. Driven by Purpose.”
